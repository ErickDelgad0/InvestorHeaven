Development Approach

Hello, my name is Erick Delgado and I am a Computer Science Major at The College of Wooster. I have developed a website application that allows investors to view real-time data from publicly traded companies. My passion for finance and technology led me to create this tool, which provides investors with an easy and convenient way to access important financial information. I began developing the application by researching the needs of investors and identifying what type of data they would find most useful. I then worked on creating an intuitive interface that would make it easy for investors to access the information they need.
